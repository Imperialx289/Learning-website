hello guys in privious part we make simple table and in this part we are going to see how make merge table so let's start....
Tag which use:-
<table> is use as opening and for closing</table>

How to use it:-
<table border=10 align="center" width=400 height=400 cellspacing=10 cellpadding=20> <caption>Merge</caption>
<tr>
<td valign=bottom rowspan=2>1</td>
<td> 2</td>
<td> 3</td>
<td> 4</td>
</tr>
<tr bgcolor=hotpink>
<td colspan=3 align=right> 6</td>
</tr>
<tr bgcolor=lime>
<td> 9</td>
<td colspan=2 rowspan=2>10</td>
<td> 12</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>13</td>
<td>16</td>
</tr>
</table>


Notice:-
<caption> attribute is use to give caption to our table.
<colspan> attribute is use to merge 2or3 column into one column .
rowspan attribute is use to merge 2or3 rows into one row


OUTPUT:-
Merge
1 2 3 4
6
9 10 12
13 16
